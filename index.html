Digital Marketplace for Kiranas

Pasale is a digital marketplace platform that provides retailers with a one-stop solution for sourcing products using a mobile app. Pasale is the first digital Business to Business (B2B) platform for mom and pop stores (Kiranas) in Nepal.

Desired volume. Wholesale price.

Pasale provides all the products at wholesale prices even at low volume. This helps retailers improve their cash-flow and diversify their inventory. Pasale helps retailers to retain customers and compete with supermarkets. Our data driven insights help retailers of all size.

Next day delivery.

Orders are fulfilled within the next day. This helps retailers to optimize their inventory according to the demand from the customers and keep their inventory lean. Retailers do not have to keep a large inventory and can restock as soon as the inventory levels decline.

Driven by data and technology.

Pasale aims to disrupt the retail supply chain and logistics using data and technology. This removes inefficiency from the market and adds value to manufacturers, retailers and end customers. Brands can market and get their products directly to retailers through Pasale.

Order via mobile phone.

Pasale provides a mobile app with an easy to use interface in Nepali and English language for exploring products, placing orders and tracking order status. We extensively focus on usability. We avoid adding bloat and un-necessary features to keep the app simple to use.
